## Serviceability Check Process

### Address Validation
1. **Initial Check**: Automated address validation against USPS database
2. **Network Check**: Query network availability database
3. **Capacity Check**: Verify network capacity at location
4. **Final Confirmation**: Manual verification for complex addresses

### Serviceability Criteria
- **Address must be**:
  - Valid business address (no PO boxes)
  - Within network coverage area
  - Accessible for installation
  - Zoned for commercial use

- **Network must have**:
  - Available capacity for requested bandwidth
  - Proper infrastructure (fiber drop, network equipment)
  - No physical obstructions

### Serviceability Results

#### Fully Serviceable
- All requested services available
- Standard installation timeline (7-14 days)
- No additional fees or requirements

#### Partially Serviceable
- Some services available, others not
- May have bandwidth limitations
- Alternative solutions offered

#### Not Serviceable
- Address outside coverage area
- Network capacity constraints
- Physical installation barriers

#### Pending Verification
- Requires site survey
- Complex installation scenario
- New construction or major renovation

## Network Performance Standards

### Bandwidth Delivery
- **Guaranteed Bandwidth**: 100% of subscribed speed
- **Measurement**: 95th percentile over monthly period
- **Testing**: Available via customer portal
- **Credits**: If sustained <90% of subscribed speed

### Latency Standards
- **Target Latency**: <10ms to network edge
- **Maximum Latency**: <30ms to network edge
- **Measurement**: Round-trip time (RTT)
- **Monitoring**: Continuous automated monitoring

### Packet Loss Standards
- **Target**: <0.1% packet loss
- **Maximum**: <1.0% packet loss
- **Measurement**: Over 24-hour period
- **Remediation**: Automatic if >0.5% sustained

### Jitter Standards
- **Target**: <5ms jitter
- **Maximum**: <20ms jitter
- **Important For**: Voice and video services
- **Monitoring**: Real-time for voice customers

## Installation Requirements

### Site Requirements

#### Physical Access
- **Required**: Clear path from street to equipment location
- **Restrictions**: 
  - Maximum 300 feet from property line
  - No underground obstacles
  - Accessible during business hours

#### Power Requirements
- **Standard**: 120V AC outlet within 6 feet of equipment
- **Backup Power**: Recommended for critical services
- **UPS**: Customer-provided for equipment protection

#### Equipment Space
- **Minimum**: 2 feet x 2 feet wall space
- **Location**: Climate-controlled area
- **Ventilation**: Adequate airflow required
- **Security**: Locked room or cabinet recommended

### Installation Process

#### Standard Installation (7-14 days)
1. **Day 1-3**: Site survey and design
2. **Day 4-7**: Permitting and coordination
3. **Day 8-12**: Outside plant construction (if needed)
4. **Day 13-14**: Equipment installation and activation

#### Expedited Installation (3-5 days)
- **Additional Fee**: $299

  - No construction required

  - Technician availability

#### Complex Installation (15-30 days)

  - New construction
  - Underground boring required
  - Multiple floors/buildings
  - Special permitting needed
- **Process**: Custom quote and timeline
