# Order Processing Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

## Order Creation and Validation

### Pre-Order Requirements
1. **Customer Qualification**
   - Valid business name and tax ID
   - Minimum 5 employees for SMB qualification
   - Valid business address
   - Credit check completed (for orders >$500/month)
   - Serviceability confirmed at installation address

2. **Required Information**
   - Company legal name
   - Business address (physical location)
   - Billing address (if different)
   - Primary contact information (name, email, phone)
   - Tax ID or EIN
   - Authorized signer information

3. **Documentation Requirements**
   - Business license or registration
   - Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement)
   - Government-issued ID of authorized signer
   - W-9 form for tax purposes

### Order Validation Checklist
- [ ] All required fields completed
- [ ] Serviceability confirmed
- [ ] Credit check passed (if required)
- [ ] Pricing approved per approval matrix
- [ ] Terms and conditions accepted
- [ ] Contract signed by authorized signer
- [ ] Installation address verified

## Order Processing Workflow

### Step 1: Order Submission
- Order submitted through sales channel (web, agent, partner)
- Automatic order ID generated (format: ORD-XXXXXXXX)
- Order confirmation email sent to customer
- Order enters "Submitted" status

### Step 2: Order Review (1-2 business days)
- **Automated Checks**:
  - Address validation
  - Duplicate order detection
  - Credit check (if required)
  - Pricing validation
  
- **Manual Review** (if flagged):
  - Large orders (>$2,000/month)
  - Custom pricing requests
  - Special installation requirements
  - High-risk credit profiles

### Step 3: Order Approval
- **Auto-Approval Criteria**:
  - Standard products only
  - Total <$500/month
  - Credit score >680
  - No special requirements
  
- **Manual Approval Required**:
  - Custom configurations
  - Discounts >10%
  - Installation challenges
  - Credit score 600-680

### Step 4: Fulfillment Initiation
- Order moves to "Processing" status
- Fulfillment job created
- Equipment reservation in inventory
- Installation scheduling begins
- Customer notified of next steps

## Order Modification Policy

### Allowed Modifications (Before Installation)
- **Product Changes**: 
  - Upgrade to higher tier: Allowed, price difference applies
  - Downgrade to lower tier: Allowed within 48 hours of order
  - Add services: Allowed anytime
  - Remove services: Allowed within 48 hours
  
- **Address Changes**:
  - Allowed if new address is serviceable
  - May require new credit check
  - Installation date may be affected
  
- **Contact Information**:
  - Allowed anytime
  - Requires verification of authorized signer

### Modification Restrictions
- Cannot modify after installation begins
- Cannot change contract terms after signing
- Cannot remove required services from bundles

### Modification Process
1. Customer submits modification request
2. Serviceability check (if address change)
3. Pricing recalculation
4. Approval required if price increases >10%
5. Updated contract sent for signature
6. Fulfillment schedule adjusted

## Order Cancellation Policy

### Cancellation Windows
- **Free Cancellation**: Within 24 hours of order submission
- **Standard Cancellation**: 24-72 hours, $50 processing fee
- **Late Cancellation**: 72+ hours, $150 fee + equipment restocking fee

### Cancellation After Installation
- Subject to early termination fees per contract
- **12-month contract**: 50% of remaining monthly charges
- **24-month contract**: 60% of remaining monthly charges
- **36-month contract**: 75% of remaining monthly charges

### Cancellation Process
1. Customer submits cancellation request
2. Cancellation fee calculated
3. Approval from sales manager
4. Fulfillment job cancelled
5. Equipment reservation released
6. Refund processed (if applicable) within 7-10 business days

## Terms and Conditions

### Standard Contract Terms
- **Minimum Contract Period**: As specified per product (12, 24, or 36 months)
- **Auto-Renewal**: Month-to-month after initial term
- **Price Lock**: Guaranteed for initial contract term
- **Early Termination**: Subject to early termination fees

### Payment Terms
- **Due Date**: 1st of each month
- **Payment Methods**: ACH, credit card, check
- **Auto-Pay**: 5% discount for ACH auto-pay enrollment
- **Late Payment**: $25 fee after 15 days, service suspension after 30 days

### Service Installation
- **Installation Window**: 8 AM - 6 PM, Monday-Friday
- **Customer Presence**: Required for all installations
- **Installation Preparation**: Customer responsible for site access
- **Installation Delays**: Customer-caused delays may incur rescheduling fee ($75)

### Equipment
- **Ownership**: Equipment remains property of service provider
- **Maintenance**: Provider responsible for equipment maintenance
- **Damage**: Customer liable for damaged or lost equipment
- **Return**: Equipment must be returned within 30 days of service termination

### Service Level Agreements
- **Uptime Guarantees**: As specified per product tier
- **Support Hours**: 24/7 for all business services
- **Response Times**: As specified in SLA
- **Service Credits**: Automatic credits for SLA violations

### Liability and Indemnification
- **Service Interruptions**: Provider not liable for force majeure events
- **Data Loss**: Customer responsible for data backups
- **Third-Party Claims**: Customer indemnifies provider for misuse
- **Limitation of Liability**: Limited to 3 months of service fees

### Termination Rights
- **Provider Termination**: 30 days notice for non-payment or breach
- **Customer Termination**: Subject to early termination fees
- **Service Discontinuation**: 90 days notice if service discontinued in area

## Order Status Definitions

### Draft
- Order created but not submitted
- Customer can modify freely
- No commitment

### Submitted
- Order submitted for processing
- Under review
- Customer notified of receipt

### Processing
- Order approved
- Fulfillment initiated
- Equipment reserved

### Fulfillment
- Installation scheduled
- Equipment shipped/prepared
- Technician assigned

### Activation
- Installation complete
- Service being activated
- Testing in progress

### Completed
- Service active
- Billing initiated
- Welcome packet sent

### Cancelled
- Order cancelled
- Equipment released
- Refund processed (if applicable)

### Failed
- Order could not be completed
- Customer notified
- Resolution options provided

## Escalation Procedures

### Order Issues Requiring Escalation
- Installation delays >5 days beyond scheduled date
- Equipment unavailability
- Serviceability conflicts
- Pricing discrepancies
- Customer disputes

### Escalation Path
1. **Level 1**: Order Processing Team (0-24 hours)
2. **Level 2**: Order Management Supervisor (24-48 hours)
3. **Level 3**: Operations Manager (48-72 hours)
4. **Level 4**: VP of Operations (72+ hours)

### Customer Communication
- Status updates every 48 hours for pending orders
- Immediate notification of delays or issues
- Proactive communication for installation scheduling
- Post-installation follow-up within 24 hours

## Quality Assurance

### Order Accuracy Metrics
- **Target**: 99% order accuracy
- **Measurement**: Orders completed without modification or error
- **Review**: Weekly audit of random sample (10% of orders)

### Customer Satisfaction
- **Target**: 90% satisfaction score
- **Measurement**: Post-installation survey
- **Follow-up**: All scores <7/10 require manager follow-up

### Processing Time Metrics
- **Target**: 90% of orders processed within 2 business days
- **Measurement**: Time from submission to fulfillment initiation
- **Escalation**: Orders pending >3 days automatically escalated
